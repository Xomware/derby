Race Expectation

Genuinely am struggling choosing between Renegade and Further Ado as Renegade is the best horse but drew a tough starting post at 1. 

Six Speed and Pavlovian will jump out in the lead to start but between the two I think Pavlovian has the better chance to maintain his pace until the end. Six Speed will die after turn 2.

Stalkers like Further Ado and Chief Wallabee will be the first ones to attack around the final turn with deep closer Renegade having to navigate a ton of traffic coming from around the 1-hole but he will have a shot at the end.

Other closers who could sneak in behind at the end to complete a trifecta or superfecta could be Golden Tempo, Incredibolt, The Puma, and Commandment.

Going to come down to dealer’s choice between Renegade or Further Ado. I will keep it simple and bet the best horse, Renegade in straights. But then use Further Ado atop in exotics.
