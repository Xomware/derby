Derby 2023

Analysis:
This race is one of the most wide open races (between 5-6 horses) and one that I don’t have a clear lean to win. The best horses in this field are all ones that will start mid-pack to dead last. With the speed horses that will press the pace at the beginning not being huge factors, there will not be a collective push on the pace. Additionally, last year’s derby had a blistering pace and historically there is a correction with a slower pace the next year. With that said, look for whichever horse can start well and be around 5th at the first turn and then make a move up to first and turn up the pace going into the second turn. I expect the pack of horses to be fairly bunched up at this point making it tough for some of these closers to get a good trip weaving through traffic. The horses I think with a good enough post and speed to start could be Derma Sotogake (a Japan horse has never won so I’m not excited about this pick) and Kingsbarns (lacks experience so not overly excited about this pick either). Forte drawing an outside post will help him be able to swing outside to make a move when he needs, so I think he will absolutely be able to finish top 3 (my main bet, will probably pay even odds to show). Angel of Empire is another horse that if he positions himself well not getting caught in the back too early I like to make a push around the final turn with a shot to win. Wide open derby, honestly haven’t felt this conflicted in a derby yet, so take these picks with a grain of salt.
